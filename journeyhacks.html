SproutfulAI: Gamifying Mental Wellness

SproutfulAI emerged from a half-day hackathon focused on mental health improvement. Our team of four recognized the need for more engaging approaches to mental well-being, leading us to develop this AI-powered app to gamify breathing exercises. Users engage in unique exercises with each login, while the rewarding feature of watering a virtual sprout incentivizes regular practice. With a sleek design and interactive elements, SproutfulAI offers a fun and effective solution for enhancing mental wellness in a short time frame.

Design Lead: Crafting Engaging Interfaces

As the design lead, I led the creation of mockups using Figma, with a focus on crafting a user-centric interface that prioritized engagement and aesthetic appeal. I collaborated closely with team members, actively listening to feedback and critiques to iterate on the design, ensuring its effectiveness in promoting mental well-being. Additionally, I played a crucial role in integrating the OpenAI API to provide more personalized and unique breathing exercises, thereby enhancing the overall functionality of the app.

From Ideation to Implementation

After thorough research and ideation, we transitioned into the design phase. Utilizing Figma, I crafted mockups for my teammates to use as a guide during the development phase. This collaborative process facilitated a seamless transition from design to code, resulting in a functional project that reflected our shared vision.

Hackathon Success: Validating Impactful Solutions

After the hackathon, our team was chosen as one of the winners, which ultimately validated our ability to deliver impactful solutions under tight deadlines. This project provided us with invaluable insights into effective teamwork and time management. Moving forward, our roadmap includes implementing further enhancements, such as user authentication and calendar pages to keep track of users' completed days. Overall, we aim to enhance user engagement and continue focusing on making a difference in mental wellness.
